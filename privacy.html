Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

What we collect

ReRadar collects no personal data. There are no user accounts, no login, no tracking cookies, and no analytics that identify individuals. All tools run entirely in your browser.

Data we use

ReRadar displays publicly available data from third-party sources including FRED, Zillow Research, Redfin, Census Bureau, SEC EDGAR, and others. This data belongs to those providers and is displayed subject to their respective terms of use.

Third-party services

  • FRED API (Federal Reserve Bank of St. Louis) — used to fetch live mortgage rate data. No personal data is sent.
  • RSS2JSON — used to convert public RSS news feeds into JSON for the news section. No personal data is sent.
  • Google Fonts — used to load typography. Subject to Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

ReRadar does not set any cookies or use local storage to track users across sessions.
